The beer brands manufactured and marketed by United Breweries Ltd. have always
been recognized for their international quality. A name synonymous with beer
in India, Kingfisher stands for excitement, youth, and camaraderie.

This largest-selling beer in India, commands a significant market share* in the
country with an alternate bottle of beer sold in India being a Kingfisher brand. We
are also available in 60+ countries across the globe.

Over the years, the Kingfisher family has expanded to include brands and variants
that cater to all segments of our audience. The Heineken brands augment our
portfolio with stellar products of global repute, making UB a preferred option for
consumers and customers alike.
